Most serious store-owners spend considerable effort on A/B testing various landing page designs, color schemes, and checkout flows. Despite this, few of us put effort into making sure our pricing is equally optimized. Finding a more profitable price for a product is one of the best ways to make your business stronger.

SweetSpot works just like regular A/B testing:

  • Select a control price and a test price for one or more products
  • Start the experiment!
  • Let the experiment run until it reaches a statistically significant result
  • Adjust the price accordingly

Statistically sound

When conducting any scientific experiment, it's essential to control for confounding variables. For example, if you change the price of a product from one week to the next, there's no way of knowing the impact of the change on your profits. It might just be that more people bought more the other week because it happened to be when they received their salaries. In this case, you might get mislead into switching to a price that may even perform worse than the original.

This is why you want to base crucial business decisions, such as the price of your product, on statistically significant results. Luckily, you don't need to be a Data Scientist to use SweetSpot! It has been built from the ground up to deliver reliable results using an easy-to-use interface.
